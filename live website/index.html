What Do We Track?

The F.A.M.platform tracks investments accross many different markets. We currently support all stocks on the United States Stock Exchange, all options on the Chicago Board of Options Exchange, and cryptocurrencies on Coinbase, Gemini, Poloniex, Kraken, Cex.io, Binance, and HitBTC. We strive to be a fully intigrated asset management solution for our members.



Why Should You Care?

Because we track assets across the most desirable investment markets, we can track the performance of virtually all of your investsments over time. We provide the convenience of full portfolio management with real time market data.
